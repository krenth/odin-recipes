Taco Meat

Description

This taco meat recipe makes the best filling for tacos. You have to buy everything else, of course. Make a big batch and use it for multiple different meals: add to bean burritos or mix with a can of beans and use for Frito pie, Navajo tacos, or taco salad.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound lean ground beef
  • 0.5 teaspoons onion powder
  • 0.5 teaspoons garlic salt
  • 0.5 teaspoons celery salt
  • 0.5 teaspoons ground cumin
  • 8-10 ounces tomato sauce

Steps

  1. Heat large skillet over medium-high heat.
  2. Cook and stir ground beef until browned and crumbly (5-7 minutes).
  3. Season beef with onion powder, garlic salt, celery salt, and cumin.
  4. Pour tomato sauce over beef.
  5. Stir to coat and simmer until slightly thickened (about 5 minutes).
