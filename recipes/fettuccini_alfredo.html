Fettuccini Alfredo

Description

This recipe is a classic fettuccini alfredo recipe that tastes like it came from the restaurants in Italy themselves. This is an easy and delishish meal to make for yourself or your family. It has a preparation time of about 40 minutes, yet tastes authentic and is easy to make. On top of all of that, this meal is very cheap to make.

Ingredients

  • 8 ounces dry fettuccine pasta
  • 4.5 tablespoons butter
  • 2.5 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 2 cups milk
  • 1 teaspoon minced garlic
  • 5 drops hot pepper sauce
  • 0.5 cups grated parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Cook pasta in a large pot of boiling salted water.
  2. Melt butter or margarine in a saucepan.
  3. Stir in flour.
  4. Whisk in milk, garlic, and hot sauce.
  5. Stir until thickened.
  6. Stir in cheese.
  7. Drain pasta.
  8. Serve sauce over noodles.
