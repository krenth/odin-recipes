Spaghetti

Description

This spaghetti dish is classic and homemade. It includes homemade spaghetti sauce with ground beef. It takes about 75 minutes to fully prepare and will yield about 8 servings. This is the perfect homemade meal for the family. It is quick, easy, delish, and more importantly it won't break the bank.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 medium, chopped onion
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 small green bell pepper, diced
  • 28 ounces diced tomatoes
  • 16 ounces tomato sauce
  • 6 ounces tomato paste
  • 2 teaspoons dried oregano
  • 2 teaspoons dried basil
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
